What is DabbaMap?

DabbaMap is India's free, community-powered directory of home cooks and tiffin providers. Every day, thousands of people across the country eat badly — not because there's no good food around them, but because they don't know how to find it. DabbaMap solves that. We make every home cook in your neighbourhood discoverable, and we help food-seekers find real, home-cooked meals at prices they can afford every day. We are not a food delivery app. We take no commission. We connect people — and the rest happens between a cook and a customer, the way it always has.

Why home-cooked food beats eating out every day

Restaurant and delivery-app food is designed to taste impressive in a single meal — rich gravies, heavy oil, high salt, large portions. Eaten daily, that takes a measurable toll on your health, your wallet, and your energy. A home tiffin is cooked the way a family cooks: fresh vegetables, moderate oil, balanced portions, and the kind of food that sustains you rather than overwhelms you.

The cost difference is equally stark. A daily meal from a delivery app costs ₹200–₹400 per order plus delivery fees — roughly ₹6,000–₹12,000 a month. A tiffin subscription for the same number of meals costs ₹2,000–₹3,500. That's a saving of ₹3,000 to ₹8,000 every month, with better food. Over a year, that gap is significant enough to be life-changing for a student or a young professional.
