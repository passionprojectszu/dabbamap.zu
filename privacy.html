Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

DabbaMap ("we", "us", "the site") connects home cooks and tiffin providers with people looking for home-cooked meals. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using dabbamap.com, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: when you sign up, we store your name, email address, and a securely hashed password (handled by our authentication provider, Supabase).
  • Listings: if you list a dabba, we store the details you provide — provider name, area, pincode, food type, cuisine, prices, description, and an approximate map location you set.
  • Messages: enquiries and replies you send through the site are stored so the other party can read them.
  • Usage data: standard technical information (such as browser type and pages visited) may be collected by our hosting and advertising partners.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and let you log in across devices.
  • To publish your listing so other users can discover it.
  • To relay messages between customers and providers.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the site.

3. Cookies and advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can learn more about how Google uses data at How Google uses information from sites that use its services.
  • For more options, visit aboutads.info.

4. How your data is stored

Account, listing, and message data is stored securely with Supabase, our database and authentication provider. Some preferences may also be stored in your browser's local storage. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Sharing your information

Your public listing details are visible to all site visitors (this is the purpose of the directory). Your email and password are never shown publicly. We share data only with the service providers needed to run the site (hosting, database, advertising) and where required by law.

6. Your choices

  • You can edit or remove your listings from your dashboard.
  • You can stop using the site at any time.
  • To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us using the details below.

7. Children's privacy

DabbaMap is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at umangdungarwal7@gmail.com.
